Our skills

A wonderful serenity has taken possession of my entire soul, like these sweet mornings of spring which I enjoy with my whole heart.

Enjoy the experience of brewing your own beer at Father John’s Brewery. Choose from a variety of recipes ranging from light beer to dark bitters. We will provide the ingredients, equipment and knowledge to make a brew you will be proud to serve at your next party. You can purchase your bottles or reuse the bottles from your last visit. You may also design your own labels or use one of our stock designs. Start by selecting a recipe that fits your style or let our brew coaches help you choose one that is right for you. You will do the measuring, mixing and cooking in our modern facility with the help of our friendly staff. Once the brew is done, we will store it in our fermentation room for a few weeks, depending on the recipe. We will filter, carbonate and keg it for you. When it is ready, you can return to bottle and label it. Take your new brew home to share with your friends. To schedule a brew session

To reserve your brewing time, call 419-633-1313. You may also fill out the contact form located on the bottom of the page and one of our team members will contact you as soon as possible during regular business hours. You may bring up to two friends or family to participate in the brewing. They must be at least 18 years old and sign a waiver as your guest. Each brew batch yields 72 22 oz. bottles.
